About

Shibboleth is a deployed natural language classifier that determines the American political party leanings of your text. Just the two major parties for now!

Our people

Jo Anna Capp received a PhD in biochemistry/biophysics from Duke University. At first reluctantly forced into statistical programming by her thesis adviser, she quickly came to love it. She's now a data nerd/stay-at-home mom who loves to test the capabilities of her laptop by performing calculations locally that should really be pushed to a cluster. Current projects include creating a character map of all the star wars movies using natural language processing, and exploring census data to understand the what's happening in the United States. Jo Anna does statistical programming in R, python, and Spark, and is actively looking for data science opportunities that take her away from the clutches of a sometimes angry mini-dictator (her toddler).
